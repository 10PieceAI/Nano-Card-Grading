Step-by-Step Guide

How Your Cards Get Graded

From the moment you submit your order to the time your slabs are back in your hands, we follow a clear, transparent process using AI-assisted analysis and expert human review. Here’s exactly what to expect.

The Nova Grading Workflow

  1. 1. Online Submission

    Start on our main website by completing the submission form. You’ll:

    • Enter your contact and return shipping information.
    • List each card (year, set, player, card number, parallels, etc.).
    • Choose your desired service level / turnaround time.
    • Confirm if you want subgrades, autograph grading, or special notes.

    Tip: Double-check spelling and card details to make population reports and future rechecks easier.

  2. 2. Prepare & Package Your Cards

    Before shipping, please package your cards safely:

    • Place each raw card into a penny sleeve first.
    • Insert sleeved cards into rigid card savers (preferred) or top loaders.
    • Stack cards together and wrap with a soft band or painter’s tape (avoid regular tape on cards).
    • Sandwich the stack between pieces of cardboard for extra rigidity.
    • Use bubble wrap or foam and place everything in a sturdy box (avoid plain envelopes).

    Do not: send cards in screw-down holders or one-touch cases; these will be removed and may risk damage.

  3. 3. Ship to Nova Grading Co.

    Ship your package to the address provided at the end of the submission form. We recommend:

    • Using a tracked shipping method (USPS, UPS, FedEx, etc.).
    • Adding insurance that matches the estimated value of your cards.
    • Including a printed copy of your submission confirmation inside the box.

    Once your tracking shows delivered, please allow a short intake window for us to log your order into our system.

  4. 4. Check-In & Order Verification

    After your package arrives:

    • We open and verify card counts against your submission list.
    • Your cards are assigned an internal order ID and secured in our grading vault.
    • Any obvious issues (missing items, damage in transit, etc.) are documented and we contact you if needed.

    At this point, your turnaround clock begins based on the service level you selected.

  5. 5. AI Pre-Grading & Imaging

    We scan and image your cards using high-resolution equipment. Our AI grading system then:

    • Analyzes corners, edges, centering, and surface details.
    • Flags print lines, whitening, creases, and other imperfections.
    • Generates a preliminary score range and notes for human graders.

    AI never replaces the grader—it provides consistent, unbiased data so our experts can focus on final judgment.

  6. 6. Human Grading & Subgrades

    Experienced graders review each card in hand, along with AI analysis. They:

    • Independently evaluate each subcategory (corners, edges, surface, centering).
    • Cross-check AI flags to confirm or correct detected defects.
    • Assign final subgrades and an overall grade based on our scale.

    Multiple graders may review high-value or borderline cards to ensure fairness and consistency.

  7. 7. Slabbing & Label Creation

    Once grades are finalized:

    • Labels are printed with set, player, grade, and subgrades (if selected).
    • Cards are sonically sealed in our custom Nova holders.
    • Each slab receives a unique ID / serial number for future verification.

    Slabs are inspected to make sure there’s no debris, misaligned labels, or case defects.

  8. 8. Quality Control & Population Logging

    Before your order ships:

    • Final QC confirms each slab’s grade matches our system records.
    • Cards are logged into our population database (where applicable).
    • We prepare your digital or printed grade summary.

  9. 9. Return Shipping to You

    Your graded slabs are packed securely for return:

    • Slabs are wrapped and separated to prevent scratching.
    • We use protective packing materials and a sturdy box.
    • Tracking information is sent to the email used on your order.

    Turnaround time depends on the service level you selected and begins at check-in, not the day you ship.

  10. 10. Viewing Your Grades & Pop Reports

    After your order is completed, you can:

    • Review your grades via the order summary we provide.
    • Look up certain cards in our population report (as it launches and expands).
    • Reference slab IDs for future crossovers, sales listings, or rechecks.

Turnaround Expectations

Turnaround times vary based on the service level you choose on the main site (e.g., Economy, Standard, Express).

  • Clock starts after check-in, not when you ship.
  • High-volume periods or special events may extend estimates.
  • We’ll contact you if any major delays occur.

Shipping & Packaging Checklist

  • ✓ Each card in a penny sleeve
  • ✓ Use card savers or top loaders (no screw downs)
  • ✓ Protect with cardboard + bubble wrap
  • ✓ Put in sturdy box (not a plain envelope)
  • ✓ Include printed submission form
  • ✓ Use tracked & insured shipping
